About Us

Smart Rentals. Made Simple.

Founded by Nimshan and Pasindu, Simple Rent was born from a shared vision: to make renting more accessible, trustworthy, and seamless for everyone in Sri Lanka. Inspired by platforms like Airbnb, we aim to connect property owners with people seeking short- or long-term stays, event spaces, or commercial rentals all in one professional, easy-to-use platform.

We're here to empower both renters and owners with tools, transparency, and local customer support you can count on.

  • Easy Listing & Booking. Property owners can list rentals in minutes, while tenants browse and book with a few clicks — no calls, no paperwork.
  • Secure Payments & Trust. All transactions are encrypted and transparent. Reviews, verified IDs, and secure messaging ensure trust on both sides.

Our Mission

To build a trusted digital platform that simplifies renting and empowers local communities.
